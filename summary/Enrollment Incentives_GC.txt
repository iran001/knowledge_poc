ENROLLMENT INCENTIVES – Quick Reference (GC) Hotel employees earn IHG One Rewards points via two programs: 1. **Enroll for Rewards (EFR)** - 300 points per Qualified Enrollment (QE) with valid email/mobile - QEs include all guest types (leisure/OTA bookings), excluding $0.00 rates - Requirements: - Enrollment within 24h pre-check-in to check-out - Linked to guest's stay at same hotel - Valid non-temporary email/mobile (verified by 3rd-party) - Not duplicate in system - Points deposited to Hotel Experience Source account after 20th of month - Quarterly bonus incentives may apply - Member benefits: Exclusive rates*, late checkout, reward nights (no blackout dates), priority recognition *Excludes Six Senses & IHG Army Hotels 2. **Ambassador Incentive** - 5,000 points (InterContinental Hotels & Resorts) or 3,000 points (other brands) per new/renewed Ambassador membership - Key benefits: - Guaranteed room upgrade & 4PM checkout - Platinum Elite status - Free weekend night & daily mineral water - F&B credit & dedicated check-in - Mainland China: Free breakfast (1 guest/night) *Mainland China excludes free weekend night & F&B credit General: - No cost to hotels for points - Points redeemable for stays, gift cards, miles, or catalog items - Guest consent required prior to enrollment - Compliance requires meeting Quarterly Enrollment Standard - 2nd Stay bonus applies only to enrollments before Jan 1, 2022 - Ambassador membership: $225 USD/year or 45,000 points (web/CRO only) (Word count: 298)
